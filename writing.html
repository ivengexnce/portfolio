"Some pages were written with shaking hands. Some were left unfinished on purpose. And some — maybe the one you're about to read — were written for you, before we even met."

This is a space for the ones who loved in silence, the ones who let go without closure, the ones who felt too deeply and told no one. And those who still carry pieces of someone they never got back.

Read slowly. Some words are soft wounds. Some might belong to you.
